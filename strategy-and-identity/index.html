During the workshop in week 1 the elements of the group found that a lot of our ideas had one thing in common. We all had the idea of changing the idea of donation. Oxfam, and many other second hand brands, all base on the idea that the costumer is responsible. It is necessary that the costumer is responsible for the brand to work. As unfortunate as it might be, there is something missing in that line of thought for a donation or second hand clothes store to succeed and compete with other larger businesses.
