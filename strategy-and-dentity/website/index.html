ABOUT

KILO® by Oxfam was concieved in the wake of the unsustainable, wasteful, and harmful nature of the fast-fashion industry. KILO® is a service designed to create a system in which little to no waste is created, hence elimnating unused clothes from ending up in landfills that pollute the earth. in addition, it encourages donations to oxfam, from which the whole community can benefit of an increase in quantity and quality of the products avaliabe for repurchase.

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

> WHERE CAN I SCALE MY ITEMS?
In any scaling station throughout london, or using or home pick up service.
 > WHERE CAN I USE THE CREDIT I RECIEVE?
You can use your credit in any store that accepts contacless payments or online payments.
 > WHEN DOES THE CREDIT APPEAR IN MY ACCOUNT?
A few seconds after you donate your clothes in a scaling station or through the pick up service the credits should come through your account.
 > HOW CAN I SCALE IF THERE IS NO STATION NEAR-BY?
You can use our convinient pick up service, that picks up and scales your clothing like in a scaling station.
 > HOW MUCH IS A KILO WORTH?
Each kilogram of items exchanged is worth 4£ of credit.
 > HOW CAN I BOOK A PICK-UP SERVICE FROM MY HOME?
You just need to fill up the form here, request a time, date and place, and voilá.
 > CAN I USE MY CREDIT IN OTHER STORES?
You can use your credit in any store that accepts contacless payments or online payments.
